<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>波士顿华埠十团舞狮庆鼠年</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-1LionDance2-e1581115006315.jpg" />
<h6>纽英中华公所“华埠醒狮迎鼠年”活动，大波士顿区政要、嘉宾、舞狮团合影。(刘景烨/大纪元)
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年02月08日讯】（大纪元记者刘景烨波士顿报导）2月2日一早，波士顿<a href="https://github.com/kmtpx253/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md">华埠</a>街道锣鼓齐鸣，鞭炮回响。各大华人狮团穿街过巷，向华埠商家拜年献瑞。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11852849" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-3LionDance2-e1581114232629.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11852849" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-3LionDance2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">中国城新年<a href="https://github.com/kmtpx253/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E7%8B%AE.md">舞狮</a>沿着大街小巷至各商家庆贺放鞭炮，非常热闹。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11852864" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-5LionDance2-e1581114745239.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11852864" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-5LionDance2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波士顿洪门致公堂首位女主席余丽k率领洪青<a href="https://github.com/kmtpx253/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%88%9E%E7%8B%AE.md">舞狮</a>团进入会场。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年，由纽英中华公所主办的“<a href="https://github.com/kmtpx253/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8D%8E%E5%9F%A0.md">华埠</a>醒狮迎<a href="https://github.com/kmtpx253/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%BC%A0%E5%B9%B4.md">鼠年</a>”舞狮庆典有10大狮团参与，包括洪青体育会、华林派、安良舞狮团等等。在Harrison大道的菲利浦广场，各狮团轮番表演，吸引众多路人驻足围观。各狮团随后挨家挨户拜访华埠商家，登门拜年。</p>
<p>波士顿市长华殊到场，感谢中华公所及狮团们保存华人传统文化。与贺嘉宾包括中华公所主席郑慧民、麻州众议员麦家威和黄子安、波士顿市议员吴弭、昆士市议长梁秀婷、侨教中心主任欧宏伟、塔夫茨医疗中心社区项目主任曾雪清等等。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11852869" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-6LionDance2-e1581114791637.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11852869" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-6LionDance2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">两位年轻的西方女士正专心剪纸。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11852871" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-7LionDance2-e1581114836424.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11852871" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-7LionDance2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员费连(后排着蓝色服装)与璞石中华文化工作室成员及访客合影。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11852873" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-8LionDance2-e1581114880408.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11852873" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-8LionDance2-600x356.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="356" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">中华公所前主席陈家骅(二排左三)和璞石中华文化工作室成员及访客合影。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11852879" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-9LionDance2-e1581114947376.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11852879" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-9LionDance2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">右起：璞石中华文化工作室会长冯文鸾、中华公所主席郑慧民、英文秘书阮鸿灿、侨教中心主任欧宏伟、中文秘书冯武成、邝珍坤合影。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在中华公所内，波士顿中华书法会和璞石中华文化工作室还带来文化村活动。不少围观舞狮的人们随后来到这里，体验中国书法、剪纸等民俗艺术。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11852845" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-2LionDance2-e1581114114646.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11852845" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-2LionDance2-600x357.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="357" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">中华公所新年文化村活动颇受欢迎的书法是弘扬正体汉字。左一为书法家黄明嘉，左三为侨教中心主任欧宏伟，右四为中华公所主席郑慧民。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11852850" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-4LionDance2-e1581114295445.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11852850" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/1-1-4LionDance2-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">波士顿市议员费连专心临摹“招财进宝”合成字。(刘景烨/大纪元)</figcaption></figure>
<p>颇受中西人士欢迎的中华公所新年文化村活动，其中书法家林卓培和黄明嘉两人一直忙碌的为索求春联、吉祥语的访客即席挥毫，也耐心指导有兴趣临摹者。波士顿市议员费连也正襟危坐的写“招财进宝”合成字。侨教中心主任欧宏伟也到场嘉许这项弘扬“波士顿地区正体汉字文化节系列活动”。</p>
<p>文化种子教师许淑芬为今年的“春”字剪纸特别增加了可爱的老鼠造型，所以难度较大，但是很受大人小孩的欢迎。◇</p>
<p>责任编辑：冯文鸾</p>
