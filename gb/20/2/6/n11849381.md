<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>祥狮献瑞钱鼠娶亲  台东监所创意花灯超吸睛</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/57e31f0e9f94a6d77bbfe53d235a1176-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>台东监所创意花灯秀。（龙芳／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年02月07日讯】（大纪元记者龙芳台湾<a href="https://github.com/ekyle2363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md">台东</a>报导）欢庆<a href="https://github.com/ekyle2363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5.md">元宵</a>，<a href="https://github.com/ekyle2363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E4%B8%9C.md">台东</a>市公所举办创意<a href="https://github.com/ekyle2363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E7%81%AF.md">花灯</a>竞赛，各监所分别做出别出心裁的花灯，设置在台东市公所前广场，主题喜庆，制作精美，夜晚点灯后，吸引许多家长带着孩子来观赏、拍照，体验元宵气氛。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11849384" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/70e92b2ce01d84b0794973eca143b110.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11849384" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/70e92b2ce01d84b0794973eca143b110-450x306.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="306" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台东监所创意<a href="https://github.com/ekyle2363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%8A%B1%E7%81%AF.md">花灯</a>秀。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11849385" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2d8cf6ac6b1a19582b953e2257b71660.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11849385" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/2d8cf6ac6b1a19582b953e2257b71660-450x347.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="347" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台东监所创意花灯秀。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>由于各监所花灯创意十足，台东市公所邀请3位评审进行评比时，难以抉择，经过热烈讨论后，才决定花灯名次：</p>
<p>第一名：泰源技能训练所－祥狮献瑞庆<a href="https://github.com/ekyle2363/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%85%83%E5%AE%B5.md">元宵</a>。</p>
<p>第二名：东成技训所－状元娶亲 鼠兆丰年。</p>
<p>第三名：岩湾技训所－财神护台湾，台东监狱－金鼠来报 迎富贵，台东戒治所－钱鼠报春迎财神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11849386" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8187c6f001f0c888d6cc0d48a5d31eb4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11849386" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/8187c6f001f0c888d6cc0d48a5d31eb4-450x387.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="387" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台东监所创意花灯秀。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11849387" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/08b2dd92970b8fad1da1d45fa593d8f8.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11849387" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/02/08b2dd92970b8fad1da1d45fa593d8f8-450x354.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="354" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台东监所创意花灯秀。（龙芳／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>市公所表示，今年是庚子金鼠年，因此创意花灯皆融入钱鼠、财神爷等概念，象征钱鼠咬钱进家门，富贵好运引进门。第一名泰源技训所的主题祥狮献瑞，则是祝福人们吉祥如意富足平安。◇</p>
