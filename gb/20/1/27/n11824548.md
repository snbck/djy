<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>BTS于葛莱美奖演出 RM曝将带来更好的专辑</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014621100707-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>防弹少年团（BTS）于第62届葛莱美奖与Lil Nas X演出照。(Kevin Winter/Getty Images for The Recording Academy )
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年01月27日讯】（大纪元记者曾薇心编译报导）第62届<a href="https://github.com/jtswrk284/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%91%9B%E8%8E%B1%E7%BE%8E%E5%A5%96.md">葛莱美奖</a>今天（当地时间26日）于洛杉矶史坦波中心举行，韩国人气男团防弹少年团（BTS）与Lil Nas X等歌手共同带来“Old Town Road All-Stars”舞台演出。防弹少年团也在红毯采访时间，透露他们本次将会带来更好的作品。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11825259" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014652100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11825259" title="BTS attend the 62nd Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014652100707-600x410.jpg" alt="BTS attend the 62nd Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="410" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）出席第62届<a href="https://github.com/jtswrk284/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%91%9B%E8%8E%B1%E7%BE%8E%E5%A5%96.md">葛莱美奖</a>红毯照。(Frazer Harrison/Getty Images for The Recording Academy)</figcaption></figure>
<p>防弹少年团去年曾以颁奖人身份参与葛莱美奖，他们今年成为首个于葛莱美奖演出的韩国歌手。在红毯采访时间，被问及美国歌手亚莉安娜（Ariana Grande）23日曾于推特上传与防弹少年团的合照一事时，防弹少年团队长RM（<a href="https://github.com/jtswrk284/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%87%91%E5%8D%97%E4%BF%8A.md">金南俊</a>）表示他们一直想与亚莉安娜合作，而亚莉安娜与防弹少年团本次也互相观赏了对方演出的彩排。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11825261" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014707100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11825261" title="BTS attend the 62nd Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014707100707-600x400.jpg" alt="BTS attend the 62nd Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团出席第62届葛莱美奖红毯照。(Emma McIntyre/Getty Images for The Recording Academy)</figcaption></figure>
<p>在被问及即将于2月发行的新专辑时，j-hope（郑号锡）以英文说道：“在听过我们的新专辑、看过我们的表演后就会知道了，喜欢BTS是你们最棒的决定。”RM表示，尽管目前还无法透露新专辑详情，但他们会带来比大家所想像的还要更好的作品。</p>
</p>
<p><center><a src=""></a><a href="https://git.io/Jvm42"><img width="600" src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jtswrk284/djy/master/gb/300/djtsp.jpg" title="进入原文观看"  alt="进入原文观看"></a><h3 align=center>(视频.国内建议下载<a href="https://git.io/JesJV">翻墙软件</a>进入原文观看)</h3><hr><a src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/AaZeTTtKvmI" width="560" b="315" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen="allowfullscreen" data-mce-fragment="1"></a></center>
<p>此外，RM在被问到“你总是作为团体的发言人，很好的回答问题，其他成员是否曾对于你的回答感到‘那不是我们想说的’呢？”一问时，RM为成员们翻译了这个问题，而成员们不假思索的一致摇着头回答“No”、“Never（不曾如此）”，RM则开玩笑说：“（若不满意我的回答）他们可能会在后台打我。”</p>
<figure id="attachment_11825267" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014606100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11825267" title="BTS attend the 62nd Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014606100707-600x362.jpg" alt="BTS attend the 62nd Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="362" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）出席第62届葛莱美奖红毯照。(VALERIE MACON/AFP via Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>RM去年7月曾与Lil Nas X合作《Seoul Town Road (Old Town Road Remix)》，本次防弹少年团全员受邀于第62届葛莱美奖，与Lil Nas X、Diplo、Mason Ramsey、Billy Ray Cyrus共同带来“Old Town Road All-Stars”合作舞台。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11825271" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014625100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11825271" title="BTS perform during the 62nd Annual Grammy Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014625100707-600x391.jpg" alt="BTS perform during the 62nd Annual Grammy Awards" width="600" b="391" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）于第62届葛莱美奖与Lil Nas X演出照。(Kevin Winter/Getty Images for The Recording Academy )</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11825270" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014521100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11825270" title="Lil Nas X and BTS perform onstage during the 62nd Annual GRAMMY Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014521100707-600x400.jpg" alt="Lil Nas X and BTS perform onstage during the 62nd Annual GRAMMY Awards" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">防弹少年团（BTS）于第62届葛莱美奖与Lil Nas X演出照。(Kevork Djansezian/Getty Images)</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，由于知名NBA球星柯比?布莱恩（Kobe Bryant）于当日因直升机失事骤逝，因此本届葛莱美奖也安排了悼念柯比的节目环节。其中，在Lil Nas X与防弹少年团等歌手带来合作舞台时，背景布景中放入了代表着柯比的洛杉矶湖人24号球衣，以悼念柯比。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11825268" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014603100707.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11825268" title="BTS perform during the 62nd Annual Grammy Awards" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/200127014603100707-600x385.jpg" alt="BTS perform during the 62nd Annual Grammy Awards" width="600" b="385" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">BTS于第62届葛莱美奖与众歌手演出时，舞台布景中有柯比的24号球衣。(Kevin Winter/Getty Images for The Recording Academy )</figcaption></figure>
