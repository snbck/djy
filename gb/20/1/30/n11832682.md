<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>东京米其林名店 预约幸福的滋味</h2>
<img width="600" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/8855d8129b1f442cf252c456f0559eab-600x400.jpeg" />
<h6>青空厚薄比例恰到好处的江户前寿司。 （业者提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2020年01月30日讯】（大纪元记者杨子樊台湾综合报导）搭上2020年东京奥运热潮，号称是“世界最多星星”的《东京<a href="https://github.com/ejouj260/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E5%85%B6%E6%9E%97.md">米其林</a>指南》日前公布最新名单，让所有饕客兴奋不已。有计划在今年到东京观看奥运竞赛的旅人们，别错过造访这些名店喔！</p>
<h2>“<a href="https://github.com/ejouj260/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md">寿司</a>之神”让每一口都出神入化！</h2>
<p>本次名单公布，让人跌破眼镜的便是连续12年获得<a href="https://github.com/ejouj260/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%B1%B3%E5%85%B6%E6%9E%97.md">米其林</a>三星评价的“<a href="https://github.com/ejouj260/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AF%BF%E5%8F%B8.md">寿司</a>之神”落榜消息。即便如此，由“寿司之神”小野二郎主理的“数寄屋桥次郎”，还是值得尝鲜者一试！</p>
<figure style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><img src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/dd8f7717d08bab70ae5e2097351e6e1d-450x675.jpeg" alt="“寿司之神”小野二郎。" width="500" b="675" /><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">“寿司之神”小野二郎。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11832694" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/c9853166e2c9c6cee4fdcb518c2e36cd.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-11832694 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/c9853166e2c9c6cee4fdcb518c2e36cd-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">数寄屋桥次郎新鲜的鲑鱼卵寿司。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>好吃理由：</h2>
<p>没有华丽的装潢，却以世界专业水准的手法，以及坚持每日新鲜亲选的食材，满足所有食客。从制作到入口的一瞬间，每个环节都是经过缜密的评估和计算，利用不同的鱼身部位，搭配精确控制温度的米饭，每一口都能吃到寿司的精髓，更曾被法国厨神侯布雄盛赞是“距离天堂最近的座位”。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832690" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5b51a8eacaf82a8cf8948ca33ce09f94.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-11832690 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/5b51a8eacaf82a8cf8948ca33ce09f94-600x360.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="360" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">银座一二岐怀石料理用餐区。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>师出同门 最高级的江户前寿司</h2>
<figure id="attachment_11832685" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/971860f46215fd86bdfff9f98bb173d0.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-11832685 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/971860f46215fd86bdfff9f98bb173d0-600x399.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">寿司名店青空。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年晋升为二星的寿司店“青空”，主厨高桥青空出自于寿司之神的门下，同样颇富盛名。</p>
<h2>好吃理由：</h2>
<p>同样坚持亲自前往市场挑选新鲜食材，完美展现鲜鱼的美味魅力，菜色因应季节食材和厨师的创意而不同，让客人可以体会时令食材真正的味道。同时店内也提供众多日本酒，配合各种煮得恰到好处的醋饭和新鲜食材，让客人们拥有最佳的美食飨宴，堪称最高级的江户前寿司。</p>
<h2>怀石料理 吃出讲究与禅意！</h2>
<figure id="attachment_11832686" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1a10bea7088e6bbf0efb0e8bbe9377cb.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-11832686 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/1a10bea7088e6bbf0efb0e8bbe9377cb-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">y留怀石料理。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>银座一二岐，结合传统与现代的日式精致怀石料理，是身为饕客绝不能错过的经典米其林美食。</p>
<h2>好吃理由：</h2>
<p>标榜不做过多的调味修饰，完美引出料理的美味。严选当地时令食材，融合传统与现代的烹饪技术，其中的烤鲣鱼使用最传统的烧稻草烹制，用烟熏去除鱼腥和提鲜，鲣鱼鲜嫩口感伴随烟熏香气，绝妙的口感让人一试难忘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832689" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/cd614bb507a0156ec92d3c15b7fc8d73.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-11832689 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/cd614bb507a0156ec92d3c15b7fc8d73-600x450.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="450" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">银座一二岐怀石料理的精致日式摆盘。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>此外，樱川怀石料理，讲求食材、料理和摆盘，精选高级食材，加上多年磨练出来的手艺发挥烹饪艺术，为食客提供极尽奢华的四季料理。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832687" style="width: 494px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/b6a28723f551afe90ab20bbbde0b1810.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-11832687 size-full" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/b6a28723f551afe90ab20bbbde0b1810.jpeg" alt="" width="494" b="350" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">樱川怀石料理。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>而世界三大知名的怀石料理店之一的“y留”，秉持的日本茶道的传统初衷，每一个微小细节都能感受到店家的细致及周到，加上主厨精湛的厨艺料理成一道道符合季节的餐点，让食客都能品尝出怀石料理随遇而安与自然和谐相处的禅道精神。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832688" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3e36695fdcfe9e931361e3b7ae47b7a9.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-11832688 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/3e36695fdcfe9e931361e3b7ae47b7a9-600x360.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="360" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">讲求艺术质感与食材美味的银座一二岐怀石料理。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>东京米其林也有法式风情</h2>
<figure id="attachment_11832691" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7c3d9e3568e5f7b4654b3ea826a39369.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-11832691 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/7c3d9e3568e5f7b4654b3ea826a39369-600x480.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">在东京也吃得到法式料理。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>除了传统日料之外，东京都惠比寿的法式料理餐厅AMOUR，也获得一星好评的推荐。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832693" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/45625c62a2ad0e7e783cc0e7cc2234f9.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-11832693 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/45625c62a2ad0e7e783cc0e7cc2234f9-600x480.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="480" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">法式料理很重视酒饮。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
<h2>好吃理由：</h2>
<p>主厨曾在法国知名餐厅工作，回国后追求创造日本独一无二的法国菜，不断的挑战与尝试，店里的每道菜都饱含主厨深切的待客之情，及让客人都赞不绝口的精湛料理手艺。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11832696" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/08e57e1d3809e9b4f18b73ea17d92693.jpeg"><img class="wp-image-11832696 size-large" src="https://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2020/01/08e57e1d3809e9b4f18b73ea17d92693-600x400.jpeg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">寿司之神（中）与数寄屋桥次郎银座本店。（业者提供）</figcaption></figure>
