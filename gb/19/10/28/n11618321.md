<h2><strong>树牵牛Ipomoea carnea 旋花科</strong></h2>
<p>又称印度旋花、树朝颜、粗茎牵牛、大本<a href="https://github.com/nevvg2846/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%B5%E7%89%9B%E8%8A%B1.md">牵牛花</a>。小灌木高可达3公尺。小枝粗壮，圆柱形或有棱，散生皮孔，密被微柔毛，实心或中空，有白色乳汁。叶宽卵形或卵状长圆形，长可达25公分，基部心形或截形，全缘，两面密被微柔毛；叶柄可长达15公分。</p>
<p>聚伞花序腋生或顶生，有花数朵或多朵。花冠漏斗状，淡红色。蒴果卵形或球形，种子4颗左右，表面被绢状长柔毛。绿化观赏用：耐光照、可栽培供观赏。乍看之下花形与一班爬藤的<a href="https://github.com/nevvg2846/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E7%89%B5%E7%89%9B%E8%8A%B1.md">牵牛花</a>非常像，仔细一看却发现花是长在枝头上，十分有趣！</p>
<figure id="attachment_11618333" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/364188_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11618333" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/364188_medium-600x446.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="446" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">叶宽卵形或卵状长圆形，长可达25公分，基部心形或截形，全缘，两面密被微柔毛；叶柄可长达15公分。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11618334" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/364189_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11618334" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/364189_medium-600x908.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="908" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">绿化观赏用：耐光照、可栽培供观赏。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11618336" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/364190_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11618336 aligncenter" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/10/364190_medium-600x906.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="906" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">花形与一般爬藤的牵牛花非常像。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
