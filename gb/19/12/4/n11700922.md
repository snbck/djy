<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>圣诞市场热闹登场 哪一个是心中最美？?</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/iStock-454402921-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>德累斯顿圣诞市场（iStock.com/LianeM） 
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年12月05日讯】（大纪元记者<span data-contrast="none">王亦笑德国报导）</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span><span data-contrast="none">进入</span><span data-contrast="none">12</span><span data-contrast="none">月份，德国各地的<a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md">圣诞市场</a>陆续开张。各大媒体也都在评选，哪个是最美圣诞市场。其实这么多年，看过这么多圣诞市场，早知道每个人心中都有一个最美名单。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="none">那或许是初来乍到，去过的第一个<a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%9C%A3%E8%AF%9E%E5%B8%82%E5%9C%BA.md">圣诞市场</a>；或许是牵着恋人的手，去过的最甜蜜圣诞市场；或许是带着父母，去过的最温馨圣诞市场。他们都是最美，美就美在那里留下了我们的身影和带有温度的记忆。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="none">如今又是霓虹闪亮、甜酒飘香季节，不如约着亲朋好友，去打卡更多有趣的圣诞市场，让他们成为</span><span data-contrast="none">2019</span><span data-contrast="none">年心中那个无法取代的“最美”！</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<h2><span data-contrast="none">以风景取胜的圣诞市场</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></h2>
<p><span data-contrast="none">这类圣诞市场，由于所在地风景太过独特，是其它圣诞市场绝对无法取代的，去这些地方打卡是观赏风景、庆祝圣诞双丰收。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="none">比如拉文纳峡谷</span><span data-contrast="none">（</span><span data-contrast="none">Ravennaschlucht</span><span data-contrast="none">）</span><span data-contrast="none">圣诞市场。在黑森林里、</span><span data-contrast="none">40</span><span data-contrast="none">米高的联排拱门下，有一个小巧精致的圣诞市场。这里</span><span data-contrast="none">景致</span><span data-contrast="none">特别</span><span data-contrast="none">，让人联想到奇异的魔法世界。虽然只有</span><span data-contrast="none">40</span><span data-contrast="none">多个圣诞摊位，但</span><span data-contrast="none">森林、峡谷、拱门打造出来的氛围却是</span><span data-contrast="none">独一无二的</span><span data-contrast="none">。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="none">同样是在黑森林，还有另一处奇特景观</span><span data-contrast="none">――</span><span data-contrast="none">飞瀑烈焰</span><span data-contrast="none">。小镇特里贝格（</span><span data-contrast="none">Triberg</span><span data-contrast="none">）的圣诞市场，以德国落差最大的瀑布为背景，</span><span data-contrast="none">飞瀑</span><span data-contrast="none">前的</span><span data-contrast="none">真人烟火秀</span><span data-contrast="none">和</span><span data-contrast="none">100</span><span data-contrast="none">万</span><span data-contrast="none">盏</span><span data-contrast="none">灯</span><span data-contrast="none">装饰的</span><span data-contrast="none">流光璀璨</span><span data-contrast="none">，都让这里如梦似幻。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="none">“</span><span data-contrast="none">蒹葭苍苍，白露为霜。所谓伊人，在水一方。</span><span data-contrast="none">”在德国</span><span data-contrast="none">基姆湖</span><span data-contrast="none">（Chiemsee）</span><span data-contrast="none">上</span><span data-contrast="none">有一座女人岛（</span><span data-contrast="none">Fraueninsel</span><span data-contrast="none">），娴雅意境倒是与这首古诗颇为契合。要去这里的圣诞市场，必须搭船前往。游湖登岛，耳畔响起古老修道院的钟声</span> <span data-contrast="none">，圣洁之感油然而生，心里充满喜乐。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="none">充满英雄主义阳刚之气的</span><span data-contrast="none">霍亨索伦</span><span data-contrast="none">城</span><span data-contrast="none">堡（</span><span data-contrast="none">Burg Hohenzollern</span><span data-contrast="none">）</span><span data-contrast="none">又是完全不同的气质，这里有辉宏雄伟建筑和</span><span data-contrast="none">野燎焰天</span><span data-contrast="none">的过往，一步步登临，仍能感受到王室豪气。城堡里的圣诞市场，无论手工艺品还是美味烤肉，都有其独到之处。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:0,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:240}">?</span></p>
<h2><span data-contrast="none">以传统取胜的圣诞市场</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:2,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:440}">?</span></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11700934" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/iStock-1082101846-e1575482162328.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11700934" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/iStock-1082101846-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text"><a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%B7%E7%B4%AF%E6%96%AF%E9%A1%BF.md">德累斯顿</a>圣诞市场（iStock.com/Tatiana Dyuvbanova）</figcaption></figure>
<p><span data-contrast="none">说到传统，那就不能不说说德国最古老的圣诞市场</span><span data-contrast="none">――</span><span data-contrast="none"><a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%BE%B7%E7%B4%AF%E6%96%AF%E9%A1%BF.md">德累斯顿</a>果脯蛋糕集市</span><span data-contrast="none">（</span><span data-contrast="none">Striezelmarkt Dresden</span><span data-contrast="none">）</span><span data-contrast="none">，</span><span data-contrast="none">其历史可追溯至</span><span data-contrast="none">1434</span><span data-contrast="none">年。果脯蛋糕</span><span data-contrast="none">是只在圣诞期间才制作的蛋糕，德累斯顿还专门有个</span><span data-contrast="none">圣诞蛋糕庆典（</span><span data-contrast="none">Stollenfest</span><span data-contrast="none">）</span><span data-contrast="none">，届时会有一个超大型重</span><span data-contrast="none">4</span><span data-contrast="none">吨以上的圣诞蛋糕穿过市区进入圣诞市场，当场切割及销售，今年的蛋糕庆典就在</span><span data-contrast="none">12</span><span data-contrast="none">月</span><span data-contrast="none">7</span><span data-contrast="none">日（周六）。</span><span data-ccp-props="{&quot;134233117&quot;:false,&quot;134233118&quot;:false,&quot;201341983&quot;:2,&quot;335551550&quot;:1,&quot;335551620&quot;:1,&quot;335559685&quot;:0,&quot;335559731&quot;:0,&quot;335559737&quot;:0,&quot;335559740&quot;:440}">?</span></p>
<p><span data-contrast="none">纽伦堡的圣婴市场（</span><span data-contrast="none">N</span><span data-contrast="none">ü</span><span data-contrast="none">rnberger Christkindlesmarkt</span><span data-contrast="none">）名声响亮，有</span><span data-contrast="none">“天下第一圣诞之城”的美誉。</span><span data-contrast="none">每当身穿盛装的“圣婴天使”展开金色翅膀，在古老露台宣布圣婴市场正式开幕之时，人们都是一片欢腾。这里的圣婴天使每年征选一次，凡是年龄在</span><span data-contrast="none">16-19</span><span data-contrast="none">岁之间，身高不低于</span><span data-contrast="none">1.60</span><span data-contrast="none">米的当地女子都可参选。在圣诞月期间，圣婴天使会定期来集市上“出巡”，能够碰到圣婴天使的小朋友，都会特别兴奋。</span></p>
