<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>车评：提升格调的R套装 2019 Volkswagen Tiguan</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>2019 Volkswagen Tiguan。〈李奥／大纪元〉
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年12月20日讯】众多的选择，有时候会令消费者难以选择，就如小型SUV市场百花齐放，几乎每一个品牌都有为这类车型推出产品，原因是购买SUV的人比购买房车的人更多，小型SUV在价格上及实用性最为合理，也即是性价比最高。Tiguan的第二代车款在去年推出市场，比上一代有显着改进，如果你也是准备购买一辆小型SUV，Volkswagen Tiguan是一款必需列入考虑清单的汽车。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">
<figure id="attachment_11734792" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_03.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11734792" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_03-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Volkswagen Tiguan。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">尾部空间比上一代增加不少。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">
<figure id="attachment_11734793" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_08-e1576822961134.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11734793" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_08-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Volkswagen Tiguan。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">R-Line套装的其中一个配件，此为19嘉宸式合金轮圈。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">
<figure id="attachment_11734794" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_11-e1576822972127.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11734794" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_11-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Volkswagen Tiguan。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">2升涡轮增压引擎，也是现时非常流行的配置，可减少耗油量之余，又可使动力输出增加，双赢方案。Volkswagen的2升涡轮增压TSI引擎，马力达到184匹及221磅张ぞ兀配以8速自动变速箱，不是DSG变速箱，应可令消费者放心吧。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">
<figure id="attachment_11734795" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_12-e1576822984450.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11734795" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_12-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Volkswagen Tiguan。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">黑与白的双色搭配，眼前一亮，但是要维持白色的座椅洁白，也是会令人苦恼，还好笔者只是一名测试者，可尽情享受。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">
<figure id="attachment_11734796" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_13-e1576822997339.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11734796" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_13-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Volkswagen Tiguan。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Panoramic power sunroof全景天窗是Comfortline或以上才有装备，让阳光照进来，当太刺眼时又可关上电动软帘。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11734797" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_16-e1576823008679.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11734797" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_16-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Volkswagen Tiguan。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11734798" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_18-e1576823021244.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11734798" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_18-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Volkswagen Tiguan。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">Tiguan是Volkswagen车款中首先采用虚弱仪表板的汽车之一，变化更多，清晰度也高。当导航地图由中控台萤幕“移动”至仪表板时，两旁的圆形仪表更会缩小一点。并且有多种不同Style设计，可能将来会让车主自已设计，例如换背景图。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">
<figure id="attachment_11734799" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_19-e1576822918729.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11734799" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_19-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Volkswagen Tiguan。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">集收音机、导航地图、蓝牙音响、手机免提连接及倒车影像等多种功能于一身的触控萤幕，位于中控台最重要位置，而汽车的恒温系统则在其下方。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">
<figure id="attachment_11734800" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption alignnone"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_22-e1576822935160.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11734800" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/12/2019_VW_Tiguan_22-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">2019 Volkswagen Tiguan。〈李奥／大纪元〉</figcaption></figure>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">变速杆设计普通，但仍有+／-手动控制，左后方是行车模式旋扭，将4Motion系统调整到最佳状态，共有四种，包括“雪地（Snow）”、“道路（On-Road）”、“越野（Off-Road）”及“越野定制（Off-Road Custom）”。</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">测试车是Highline版，再加上R-Line套装，不P钢脚踏、皮革包裹的方向盘、19己辖鹇秩ΑR-Line饰牌等等，令Tiguan更高贵更高享受。另外，比同级对手更强的，是Tiguan有提供七座位版，虽然第三排座位是比较狭窄，但有时候就是需要它呢！</p>
<p style="font-weight: 400;">文：李奥<br />
责任编辑：钟义云</p>
