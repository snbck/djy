<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>制作晶莹剔透的手工皂：蓝紫星光宝石皂</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/20191127_sally_Soap_01-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>手工制作的蓝紫星光宝石皂。（采实文化 ）
</h6>
<hr>
<p><span style="color: #000000;">将透明皂基加热后，利用不同的色水调和出各式皂色，再利用一些<a href="https://github.com/yhmwf2130/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%8A%80%E5%B7%A7.md">技巧</a>和修皂方式，制作出晶莹剔透、闪闪发亮的皂款，外型有如宝石般透亮，因此被称为“<a href="https://github.com/yhmwf2130/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E7%9F%B3%E7%9A%82.md">宝石皂</a>”。有别于以往雾面的手工皂，宝石皂造型吸睛、制作简单，成为全球皂圈竞相创作的皂款。</span></p>
<h2><span style="color: #008000;">蓝紫星光<a href="https://github.com/yhmwf2130/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%AE%9D%E7%9F%B3%E7%9A%82.md">宝石皂</a></span></h2>
<p><strong>【材料】</strong></p>
<p>透明皂基? 120gM红色色水? 1滴<br />
蓝色色水? 1滴M金色色粉? 适量</p>
<p>【模型】 7×7cm方形模</p>
<h3><span style="color: #0000ff;">作法</span></h3>
<p>1.将120g的透明皂基加热融解后，在表面喷上酒精，以消除多余气泡。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P59-1.jpg"><img class="wp-image-11684787 size-small" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P59-1-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="200" /></a>? ? <a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P59-2-1.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-small wp-image-11690237" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P59-2-1-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="200" /></a></p>
<p>2.将30g的透明皂液沿着模型边缘倒入，在表面喷上酒精，以消除多余气泡。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P59-4-1.jpg"><img class="alignleft wp-image-11690165 size-small" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P59-4-1-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="200" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P59-3-1.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-small wp-image-11690172" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P59-3-1-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="200" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>3.滴入1滴红色色水、1滴蓝色色水。</p>
<p><img class="alignnone wp-image-11684816 size-small" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P60-1-300x200.jpg" alt="蓝紫星光宝石皂的作法。（图/采实文化提供）" width="300" b="200" /></p>
<p>4.在已加热融解的透明皂液里，撒入少许金粉，并轻轻摇晃容器，使金粉自然扩散开来。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P60-2-1.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-small wp-image-11690183" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P60-2-1-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="200" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>5.将含有金粉的皂液倒在色水上，使红色和蓝色色水自然的混合扩散，形成些许的紫色。</p>
<p><img class="alignleft wp-image-11684814 size-small" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P60-3-300x200.jpg" alt="蓝紫星光宝石皂的作法。（图/采实文化提供）" width="300" b="200" /></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>6.在表面喷上酒精，以消除多余气泡。静置约20分钟后，待皂固定成型即可脱模。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P60-4.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-small wp-image-11690185" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P60-4-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="200" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P60-5.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-small wp-image-11690188" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P60-5-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="200" /></a></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>7.视个人喜好切成想要的宝石皂大小。我会先将正方形的皂切成大小不一的两块，再将较平面的边斜切出角度来，制作出宝石的折射感。</p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P61-1.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-small wp-image-11690191" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P61-1-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="200" /></a></p>
<p><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P61-2.jpg"><img class="alignleft size-small wp-image-11690194" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/11/P61-2-300x200.jpg" alt="" width="300" b="200" /></a></p>

<p>技巧①<br />
