<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>许其正：度过</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2018/10/sunset-1591599_960_720-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>（公有领域）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>长途跋涉而来<br />
爬到这里<br />
土质变松软了<br />
人也已涂上了黄昏色彩<br />
并且疲惫不堪，摇摇欲坠<br />
　<br />
但不能放弃<br />
底下虽是万丈深渊<br />
还是要戮力前往<br />
那是个命定非去不可的地方！<br />
　<br />
前程虽茫茫不可测<br />
他却坚持着<br />
一步步，踩稳脚<br />
小心翼翼，爬上去<br />
冀能度过凶险<br />
继续向前<br />
虽千难万苦，吾往矣！<br />
　<br />
坚毅强忍<br />
可以履险如夷<br />
再多再大的凶险<br />
都不怕<br />
都要度过</p>
<p><strong> Get Across </strong> &emsp;Hsu ChiCheng</p>
<p>Trudges over a long distance<br />
To crime to here<br />
The soil turns into soft<br />
Body is also smeared with colour of dusk<br />
And exhausted, and on the verge of collapse</p>
<p>However he can’t give off<br />
Though it’s a bottomless chasm<br />
He needs to join hands to struggle forward all the same<br />
It’s a place we can’t but to go destined by fate!</p>
<p>Though it’s confused and without a clue of the future<br />
He insists<br />
To goes forward continually<br />
He treads firmly, step by step<br />
Climbs on, cautiously<br />
Wishing to trudge over the danger<br />
I’ll go though there are multiple difficult!</p>
<p>With firm, and is hardhearted enough to do<br />
Can handle a crisis without difficulty<br />
How largest and most is the danger<br />
Will don’t fear at all<br />
And will trudge over@</p>
