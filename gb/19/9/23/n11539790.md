<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>旧金山韩国料理 这些年我们接触的韩餐（三）</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/15c74c7ecf0b3f1f_ttl7dayp65_15c55167c9e8d67a_ttl7dayMsN_shutterstock_1188617077-3-600x400.jpg" />
<h6>韩式鸡蛋糕。（Shutterstock）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年09月23日讯】文：李沁一?大纪元<br />
吹拂华人圈二十多年的“韩风”，展现了灿烂多姿的韩国文化：韩国名校成均馆大学、首尔-釜山旅游、韩国Hip hop街舞、多彩华丽的韩服、浪漫唯美的韩剧、万人尖叫的韩国欧巴……还有，传承韩国文化的韩餐！</p>
<p>韩餐又名韩国料理，以清淡为主，少油腻，多有辣。很多韩国料理都易食、易做，本文将为您介绍<a href="https://github.com/asdfgt5/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%BC%8F%E7%AE%80%E6%98%93%E4%BE%BF%E5%BD%93.md">韩式简易便当</a>、<a href="https://github.com/asdfgt5/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%BC%8F%E9%B8%A1%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md">韩式鸡蛋糕</a>、<a href="https://github.com/asdfgt5/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%BC%8F%E7%82%B8%E9%B8%A1.md">韩式炸鸡</a>、韩式炒年糕的做法。</p>
<h2>一、<a href="https://github.com/asdfgt5/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%BC%8F%E7%AE%80%E6%98%93%E4%BE%BF%E5%BD%93.md">韩式简易便当</a></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11556359" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/15c74c7ecf09acc7_ttl7day4Jc_15c5516668887792_ttl7day96a_shutterstock_543330511-2.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11556359" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/15c74c7ecf09acc7_ttl7day4Jc_15c5516668887792_ttl7day96a_shutterstock_543330511-2-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">韩式简易便当。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>用少量油煎蛋15秒，香肠切成小段，在锅中翻炒至熟，喜欢烧烤味道的也可以爆火炒至有点点焦，放在白米饭上（便当盒最好是平底，带盖子）。一勺韩式辣酱，抹在米饭上，将煎好的煎蛋放在辣酱上，放入韩式泡菜、海苔丝，盖上碗盖子，用力上下摇晃，再打开盖子，当当当！一盒美味的韩式便当做好啦！</p>
<p>带着便当，像具俊表、金丝草一样享受Camping的乐趣吧！</p>
<h2>二、<a href="https://github.com/asdfgt5/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%BC%8F%E9%B8%A1%E8%9B%8B%E7%B3%95.md">韩式鸡蛋糕</a></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11556361" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/15c74c7ecf0b3f1f_ttl7dayp65_15c55167c9e8d67a_ttl7dayMsN_shutterstock_1188617077-3.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11556361" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/15c74c7ecf0b3f1f_ttl7dayp65_15c55167c9e8d67a_ttl7dayMsN_shutterstock_1188617077-3-600x386.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="386" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">韩式鸡蛋糕。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>在碗中放少许面粉，打1只鸡蛋，120cc的牛奶，35克糖，泡打粉1小勺，半勺香草精，无盐奶油1大勺，用力搅拌均匀。在蛋糕的模子里刷两层食用油，倒入刚才搅拌好的食材，再在每个蛋糕模子里打一只鸡蛋。</p>
<p>烤箱200度，15分钟后，街头售卖的韩式鸡蛋糕完成！</p>
<h2>三、<a href="https://github.com/asdfgt5/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E9%9F%A9%E5%BC%8F%E7%82%B8%E9%B8%A1.md">韩式炸鸡</a></h2>
<figure id="attachment_11556362" style="width: 450px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/15c74c7ecf7416f7_ttl7dayC79_15c55172a1a0fd72_ttl7dayO6j_shutterstock_1458029153-4.jpg"><img class="size-medium wp-image-11556362" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/15c74c7ecf7416f7_ttl7dayC79_15c55172a1a0fd72_ttl7dayO6j_shutterstock_1458029153-4-450x674.jpg" alt="" width="450" b="674" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">韩式炸鸡。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>鸡肉切成块，倒入酱油、五香粉、米酒、1匙蚝油等佐料，搅拌均匀，腌一个晚上，裹上地瓜粉，油锅炸熟至金黄色，捞出。</p>
<p>平底不沾锅倒入韩式辣酱，1勺半的糖，3匙水，加入刚才炸好的鸡肉块，翻炒入味，倒到盘子里，最后撒上白芝麻。饱满多汁、外酥内软的韩式炸鸡做好了！《来自星星的你》中，千颂伊最喜欢吃的炸鸡就是这个味！</p>
<h2>四、韩式炒年糕</h2>
<figure id="attachment_11556367" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/15c74c7ecf07189f_ttl7dayz7b_15c551652298d4b2_ttl7daysqF_shutterstock_1332492647-1.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11556367" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/15c74c7ecf07189f_ttl7dayz7b_15c551652298d4b2_ttl7daysqF_shutterstock_1332492647-1-600x400.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="400" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">韩式炒年糕。（Shutterstock）</figcaption></figure>
<p>先煮干昆布、鱼干高汤备用。调制韩国辣酱，韩国辣椒酱3汤匙，辣椒粉、酱油、蜂蜜、糖各1汤匙，再加入切碎大蒜，搅拌后备用。在平底锅加入洋葱、葱段爆香，爆香完加入高汤煮滚，再加入调制好的辣酱，约中火煮5分钟，后加入年糕、鱼板，继续中火煮15分钟，接着收汁，出锅后撒上葱花。亲民、经典的韩式料理――炒年糕，做好了！</p>
