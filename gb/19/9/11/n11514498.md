<h6>眼纹拟蛱蝶Junonia lemonias aenaria 蛱蝶科。（图／郑清海提供）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p style="text-align: center;"><strong>寻蜜访花草丛绕</strong><br />
<strong>眼纹欺敌是高招</strong><br />
<strong>双双对对秀恩爱</strong><br />
<strong>身形优雅最美妙</strong></p>
<figure id="attachment_11514510" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/360786_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11514510" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/360786_medium-600x898.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="898" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">前翅具一枚橙色的大眼纹，内有黑色及白色的斑点，后翅表面有2枚一大一小的拟眼纹，翅缘具白色的波浪斑纹，翅腹面颜色较浅，斑纹与翅面同。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h2><strong>眼纹拟蛱<a href="https://github.com/woywz155/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9D%B6.md">蝶</a> Junonia lemonias aenaria蛱蝶科</strong></h2>
<p>又称麟纹拟蛱<a href="https://github.com/woywz155/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E8%9D%B6.md">蝶</a>、蛇眼蛱蝶。展翅45~55mm，翅膀表面暗褐色，前翅具一枚橙色的大眼纹，内有黑色及白色的斑点，后翅表面有2枚一大一小的拟眼纹，翅缘具白色的波浪斑纹，翅腹面颜色较浅，斑纹与翅面同。平地至低海拔山区常见，幼虫以爵床科的植物寄主，成虫常见于开阔的地面或溪边活动，喜爱访花、吸水及吸食动物排遗，雄虫具领域性强。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11514509" style="width: 600px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/360784_medium.jpg"><img class="size-large wp-image-11514509" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/360784_medium-600x399.jpg" alt="" width="600" b="399" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">幼虫以爵床科的植物寄主，成虫常见于开阔的地面或溪边活动。（图／郑清海提供）</figcaption></figure>
