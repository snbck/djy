<tr><td VALIGN=TOP width="626"><h2 align=center>The Taste美食展 台湾魅力成焦点</h2>
<img src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/9b52a7a25adf4c8d0e027fe0e060f72d.jpg" />
<h6>中华民国交通部观光局驻洛杉矶办事处9月1日参加The Taste美食展，与合作厂商将台湾元素推及主流。（袁玫／大纪元）
</h6>
<hr>
<p>【大纪元2019年09月03日讯】（大纪元记者袁玫<a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md">洛杉矶</a>报导）9月1日，中华民国交通部观光局驻洛办事处参加《洛杉矶时报》举办的年度<a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/the-taste%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F%E5%B1%95.md">The Taste美食展</a>，特别带来<a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md">台湾美食</a>及台湾优质茶品，在开拓主流民众对台湾美食认知的同时，让台湾文化元素呈现派拉蒙影城。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11495176" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/eadebd124f42c7d857b09644c1227e0b.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11495176" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/eadebd124f42c7d857b09644c1227e0b-600x792.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="660" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">交通部观光局驻<a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E6%B4%9B%E6%9D%89%E7%9F%B6.md">洛杉矶</a>办事处主任施照辉（左）与副主任洪幸慈（右）9月1日参加<a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/the-taste%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F%E5%B1%95.md">The Taste美食展</a>。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11495168" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/4ef2976cef26791f54fdd098ded0bf56-e1567480222722.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11495168" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/4ef2976cef26791f54fdd098ded0bf56-e1567480222722-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="667" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">派拉蒙影城。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>今年的美食展，观光局邀请在全球有24家店的<a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%B2%B8%E7%82%B9.md">台湾沸点</a>餐厅，为民众带来极具特色风味的小火锅试吃，该餐厅总裁张炳权表示，他们店内的有机食品和饮料是从台湾进口，力求在食物素材的保证。而参加美食展希望将台湾好汤火锅介绍给主流。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11495178" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/b9eb04d1b416d81835cd970568920eb3.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11495178" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/b9eb04d1b416d81835cd970568920eb3-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="667" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">台湾餐厅沸点总裁张炳权。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11495180" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/baf69a073e465564331d38d0240454ef.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11495180" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/baf69a073e465564331d38d0240454ef-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="667" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">观光局邀请在全球有24家店的<a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E6%B2%B8%E7%82%B9.md">台湾沸点</a>餐厅，为民众带来极具特色风味的小火锅试吃。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>另外，天仁茗茶加州茶栈也是第6年与观光局异业合作，展会中不仅分享台湾的优质茶品，推广台湾享誉国际的尝茶文化，同时还提供三种茶饮让民众试喝。茶栈副总经理高启富说明，今年特别推出200个闪亮发光杯送给现场民众，并制作最受南加民众喜爱的芒果绿茶、桂花乌龙茶及珍珠奶茶爆米花等，让民众在现场拿到发光杯，随时都能享受台湾的好茶道。</p>
<figure id="attachment_11495194" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/ea81cb93c64d839d9424052ace54a9a9.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11495194" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/ea81cb93c64d839d9424052ace54a9a9-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="667" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">天仁加州茶栈副总经理高启富。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<figure id="attachment_11495200" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/f60c61a5e0d780fba555e6dd5c73bd30.jpg"><img class=" wp-image-11495200" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/f60c61a5e0d780fba555e6dd5c73bd30-600x800.jpg" alt="" width="500" b="667" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">天仁加州茶栈特别准备200个闪亮发光杯及新产品爆米花给现场民众。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
<p>观光局每年的美食展都竭尽心思来推广不一样的世界闻名的<a href="https://github.com/liczai2340/djy/blob/master/gb/tag/%E5%8F%B0%E6%B9%BE%E7%BE%8E%E9%A3%9F.md">台湾美食</a>，驻洛办事处主任施照辉表示，今年重金打造扭蛋机，6个不同颜色的球代表台湾6种不同特色，用客家特色的花布装饰，希望针对美国的客群来品尝台湾美食的同时，借机推动台湾10月的旅游，让民众从吸睛的互动游戏认识台湾小镇旅游。他欢迎民众畅游台湾，品尝道地美食。◇</p>
<figure id="attachment_11495202" style="width: 500px" class="wp-caption aligncenter"><a href="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/bf38337f7518090b453f151b80f58383-e1567481253628.jpeg"><img class=" wp-image-11495202" src="http://i.epochtimes.com/assets/uploads/2019/09/bf38337f7518090b453f151b80f58383-e1567481253628-600x800.jpeg" alt="" width="500" b="667" /></a><figcaption class="wp-caption-text">今年重金打造的扭蛋机，6个不同颜色的球代表台湾6种不同特色。（袁玫／大纪元）</figcaption></figure>
